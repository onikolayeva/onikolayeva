Painting/ Dance

I have been dabbling in using micropigment ink and watercolors to create an array of dancers. My new series/ experimentation looks into nature and watercolors to bring bold lines and soft colors to look at the juxtaposition of the delicate yet bold state of nature. I am also interested in learning procesing and using it together with real time dance techniques.
