Painting/ Dance

I have been dabbling with micropigment ink and watercolors to create an array of dancers. My new series looks into nature by using watercolors with the ink of the pen to look at the juxtaposition of the delicate yet bold state of nature. I am also interested in learning procesing and using it with dance choreography.
